Kaleidoscope ASCII

Fast kaleidoscope mirror and ASCII art engine for Reeldemo promos, social ads, and landing visuals. Sub-millisecond renders at terminal scale. Try the flags below and watch the loop update live.

Live demo

Same options as the Rust engine and CLI: segments, rotation, density, contrast, brightness, and invert. Upload your own still or pick a stock sample. The loop runs slow by default so you can read the symmetry.
